What is the difference between raster and vector graphics?
Vector resizes more easily due to being a list of relatively placed points instead of exlpicitly placed pixels.

What are the advantages of SVG over other graphical methods in the browser?
Responsive Design, as SVG is "scalabe" and therefore will be more asily changed to different sizes without appearing to lose resolution
